FAQ

Questions, answered.

Is this legal advice?+

No. Conditio flags risks and explains clauses in plain English so you can make informed decisions — or know when to call a lawyer. It doesn't replace one.

What file types can I upload?+

PDF and DOCX. Drag and drop or upload directly — no formatting or templates required.

Is my data private?+

Yes. Your contracts are never used to train AI models. They're encrypted and visible only to you and the people you invite.

How accurate is it?+

Conditio applies the same risk rubric to every contract for consistent flags. Treat it as a thorough first-pass review — not a substitute for legal counsel on high-stakes deals.

Can I cancel anytime?+

Yes. Plans are monthly — cancel whenever you like. Your contract history stays accessible on the free tier.

What types of contracts can Conditio analyze?+

Any written contract in English: SaaS agreements, NDAs, vendor contracts, freelance SOWs, employment contracts, leases, and more. If it's a PDF or DOCX, Conditio can read it.

How long does an analysis take?+

Most contracts are analyzed in under 30 seconds. Longer documents may take up to a minute. You'll see a progress indicator while it runs.

Do you support languages other than English?+

You can upload contracts in any language and receive the report in your preferred language. The app supports 25 languages — set yours in account settings.
