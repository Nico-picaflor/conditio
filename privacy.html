Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 11, 2026

Conditio ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our AI contract risk analysis service at conditio.org.

1. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account information: Your email address when you register or sign in with Google.
  • Contract files: PDF or DOCX files you upload for analysis. These are processed in real time and are not stored permanently on our servers.
  • Analysis results: The risk scores, flags, and summaries generated from your contracts are stored in your account history.
  • Usage data: Basic analytics such as pages visited and actions taken, collected via Google Analytics.

2. How We Use Your Information

  • To provide and improve our contract analysis service.
  • To store your analysis history so you can access previous results.
  • To authenticate you and manage your account.
  • To send you important updates about the service (no marketing emails without your consent).

3. Your Contracts Are Private

We take the confidentiality of your contracts seriously:

  • Your contract files are never shared with third parties.
  • Your contracts are never used to train AI models.
  • Contract text is sent to Anthropic's Claude API for analysis under their data processing agreement, which prohibits using customer data for training.
  • Only you can access your analysis history — we enforce row-level security in our database.

4. Data Storage and Security

Your data is stored in Supabase (EU West, Ireland). We use industry-standard security practices including HTTPS encryption for all data in transit and at rest.

5. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Supabase — database and authentication
  • Anthropic Claude API — AI analysis engine
  • Google Analytics — anonymous usage statistics
  • Google OAuth — optional sign-in method

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, or delete your personal data at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at the email below. If you delete your account, all your data including analysis history will be permanently removed.

7. Cookies

We use essential cookies for authentication and Google Analytics cookies for anonymous usage tracking. You can disable analytics cookies in your browser settings.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by email or by posting a notice on our website.

9. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: support@conditio.org
